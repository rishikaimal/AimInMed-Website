Who are we?

With the rich and continued experience of more than 25 years in Medical devices design and development, and having more than 100 products being designed, developed and most of them being manufactured and used by the prestigious institutes and key-opinion-leaders of the medical fraternity across India and also by other countries, AIM team is capable of taking on the challenges of newer medical device designs and development.
